ðŸ›’ Group Buying Terms

How Drops Work

eStreamly's group buying platform allows consumers to unlock better prices as more people join a campaign ("drop"):

  1. Join a drop by providing your phone number and email
  2. Share your unique referral link with friends
  3. As more buyers join, everyone unlocks better prices
  4. When the drop ends, you'll receive your final unlocked price and checkout instructions

Pricing Tiers

Each drop has multiple price tiers based on buyer count:

  • Initial Price: The starting price when the drop begins
  • Tier Prices: Lower prices unlocked at specific buyer milestones (e.g., 100, 500, 1000 buyers)
  • Best Price: The lowest possible price, typically unlocked via referrals or high participation

All prices are in USD unless otherwise specified. The price you pay is determined at the end of the drop based on total participation.

Referral Program

You can unlock the best price instantly by referring friends:

  • Each drop specifies how many referrals are needed to unlock the best price
  • Referred friends must join using your unique referral link
  • Referrals are tracked and verified automatically
  • Once you reach the referral threshold, you'll be notified immediately via SMS

Referral requirements may vary by campaign. Abuse of the referral system (fake accounts, bots, etc.) will result in disqualification.

Checkout & Coupon Codes

When a drop ends:

  1. You'll receive an SMS with your unlocked price and a unique coupon code
  2. Coupon codes are typically valid for 4 hours after the drop ends
  3. Use the provided checkout URL and apply your coupon code to complete your purchase
  4. Expired coupons cannot be extended or reissued

All purchases are completed through the brand's own checkout system. eStreamly facilitates the group discount but does not process payments.
